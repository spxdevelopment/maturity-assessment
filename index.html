Uncover the strengths and opportunities in your go-to-market strategy. Before you begin, review the five-level scale used in this assessment:

  • None (1) – No formal process in place.
  • Emerging (2) – Early‑stage practices, limited consistency.
  • Basic (3) – Defined processes, moderate adoption.
  • Advanced (4) – Mature, optimized practices in place.
  • Leading (5) – Proven, industry‑leading excellence.
